God Jesus Holy-Spirit (GJHS)

PRAYER

Lord Jesus Christ, I believe that you are the son of the living God, the only way to God, the one who died for my sins and rose again from the dead. Hallelujah to your name Lord Jesus.

I humble myself before you. I give you thanks for who you are, myself, everyone, and everything.

Please give me the Holy Spirit for my guide and comfort.

I confess all of my sins; I apologize for them, I renounce them, and I repent of them. I also renounce every contact with satan and all evil. I denounce all the sins of my ancestors.

I forgive anyone who has hurt or wronged me and I bless that person. I also forgive myself. And I ask for your forgiveness Lord Jesus. I believe you have forgiven me and I thank you. Please wash me clean with your blood Lord Jesus.

I thank you Lord that you were made a curse; and I am now free from every curse and receiving my blessing. If I have any object that brings a curse, I ask you to reveal it to me so I can destroy it.

If I'm not a child of God, please accept me as a child of God; and I accept myself as a child of God.

Please help and guide me to fear God and be obedient. Please protect and deliver me from satan and all evil. Please heal me of all my wounds; and give me health to all my flesh. I also ask for wisdom, knowledge, and understanding; and I thank you Lord Jesus.

For all power has been given unto you in heaven and on earth. In your name Lord Jesus I pray, Amen.
